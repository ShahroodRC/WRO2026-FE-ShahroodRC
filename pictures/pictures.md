## Pictures
This folder contains photographs and diagrams of the robot and its components. Images here are referenced from the main `README.md` and are organized into subfolders:

- **electrical-diagram/** — circuit diagrams and wiring images used for assembly and documentation.
- **robot-components/** — close-up photos of mechanical parts, sensors, and modules.
- **randomizer-screenshots/** — screenshots and images produced by testing utilities and tools.

Use these images for documentation, presentations, and the project README. Filenames provide quick context (for example: `pixy-cam-wiring.jpg`, `leds-setup.jpg`, `fan-setup.jpg`).
